About The Restaurant

Lutong Nanay, or Lutong ina, originated from the heart of Filipinos. The true taste of home that everybody longs for. Our restaurant strives to fulfill your longing and give you the nostalgia of your family member's cooking with love.

Filipino Cuisines

What are Filipino cuisine's known for? Filipino cuisine is popular for its delicious taste and appetizing aroma. You can easily tell apart Filipino food from other cuisines due to its color and the manner it is served too. Its distinctive colors, aroma, and flavors result in a full sensory experience with each bite.

Filipino Beverages

Pinoys have sweet tooth, you can also expect that almost all these are served cold with ice or made into shake coolers perfect for the summer weather. To make things extra interesting, some of these drinks also include a syrup of your choice, brown sugar, sago pearls (starch from palm trees similar to tapioca pearls), or condensed milk.Filipinos are very particular about preserving their heritage, so this means that some of the concoctions have been influenced by certain details about the country.

Filipino Desserts

You may have heard of halo-halo and ube, but how many more Filipino desserts can you name? Unlike other cuisines that offer light and refreshing treats, Filipino desserts are often made from rice and starch crops. They tend to be heavy on the tummy, which makes them perfect for breakfast (agahan) or an afternoon snack (merienda). Filipinos are dessert-lovers, so they have a myriad of mouthwatering treats to offer.
